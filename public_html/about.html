About ShareLive

ShareLive was created by Benjamin Howe at BrumHack 5.0 (29th - 30th October 2016) over 25 hours. It was inspired by an article published in the "Frontiers in Psychology" journal by researchers at the University of Oxford about how sharing the experience of listening to music can increase social bonding.
